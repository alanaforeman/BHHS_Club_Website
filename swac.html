Student Wellness Advisory Committee

SWAC is designed to inspire students to live their best lives through the lens of overall wellness. This club aims to educate students on practical methods for addressing their physical, emotional, and social wellness needs. This special club will offer students the opportunity to document their experiences, set personal wellness goals, and revitalize the way they eat, move, and live.

1x a week from 2:30-3:30
