Model United Nations

Model UN is a group of globally minded students with an interest in other countries, current events, and solving world problems. Students in this club have the opportunity to attend a four day Harvard Model United Nations simulation in January in Boston. Delegates gain insight into the workings of the United Nations and the dynamics of international relations by assuming the roles of UN representatives and members of other international bodies and national cabinets of your choice. We also participate in other one-day local conferences scattered throughout the year, for those looking for less of a time commitment than HMUN.
