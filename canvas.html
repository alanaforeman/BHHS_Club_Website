The Canvas Literary and Art Magazine

The Canvas is Byram Hills High School’s art and literary magazine. Twice a year we publish art and writing pieces created by our own students, allowing them an opportunity to be recognized for their amazing work. As a member of our club, you would help us format and edit submissions, create posters, and plan ways to spread the word about The Canvas.

If you enjoy graphic or web design, reading different pieces of writing, or are a creative individual, The Canvas is the perfect club for you. There are plenty of opportunities for taking on leadership roles and proposing new ideas. These opportunities could include cultivating our social media presence, planning a poetry event for National Poetry Month, or designing posters to encourage submissions. We sincerely hope that you join us in our mission of fostering creativity at The Canvas!

We meet in Mrs. Aber’s room (Room 321) every two weeks.
