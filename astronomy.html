The Astronomy Club

Have you ever wanted to see Saturn’s rings, or perhaps Jupiter’s Great Red Spot? At the Astronomy Club’s observatory nights, you can. We also host movie screenings, so come watch your favorite space movie. Members are also encouraged to teach the club on a topic of their choice. Anyone interested in astronomy, using the telescope, perhaps just wants to watch some movies, or just wants to meet other passionate people is welcome to join.

The Astronomy Club will meet on Thursdays, every other week. Meetings will be held in Mr. Covert’s classroom (room 328), and on occasion outside!

The Google Classroom code is b743bzv.
