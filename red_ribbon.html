Red Ribbon

Red Ribbon is a social advocacy club focused around bringing awareness and attention to the cause of HIV and AIDS, both here in the United States and abroad. We do bake sales, educate through World AIDS day and the flag project, and we raise money through the NYC AIDS walk in May. Join us in making a difference, both locally and internationally. We have fun while doing good.

Meetings will be after school at 2:30, lasting 20 to 30 minutes. The day of the week is still TBD. They are in room 413.

Set your calendar for the third Sunday in May for the AIDS walk!
