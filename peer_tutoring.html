Peer Tutoring Club

Peer Tutor is a volunteer club for students to work with other students on their academics. Typically, upperclassmen whom have excelled in an earlier class will work with underclassmen once or twice a week. This help can be weekly or just prior to examinations. Itâ€™s a way to connect students with one another as well.

We are looking for juniors and seniors who have performed well in their earlier classes. We will align you with a younger student to not only help but to also serve as a Big Brother/Big Sister to them. There is a spreadsheet that lists what subject each Peer Tutor specializes in, and once they have a tutoree, the appointments will be scheduled between the two students.

All meetings take place virtually between club members and tutoring sessions are set up independently. Students and their assigned tutors can meet in person provided they follow Covid Protocols.

If you are interested in joining the club as a sophomore, please see Mr. Allen (Room 411/ mallen@byramhills.net) or Alyssa Margolin (margolina22@byramhills.net).
