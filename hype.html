Helping Young People Excel

HYPE (Helping Young People Excel) is a service club with the goal to foster an understanding and awareness of those members of the community with special needs. Members of HYPE do this by hosting online and in-person events with various groups throughout Westchester County and NYC. Last year's events, included virtual BINGO parties, a holiday gift drive, and Valentine's Day care packages. New members are always welcome and we look to expand our reach to new organizations each year. Meetings will be held monthly.
