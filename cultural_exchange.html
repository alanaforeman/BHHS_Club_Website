The Cultural Exchange Club

The Cultural Exchange Club is all about expanding our global awareness and learning more about different cultures. Come and share something cool about your own culture or learn something new about your friends! In our club, we research and learn about the traditions and lifestyles of other nations, share traditional or modern recipes, music, poetry, and more. In past years, we have been featured in Yearbook and the Oracle articles, and have presented at the Global Scholars Vox Summit in the spring!
