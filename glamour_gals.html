Glamour Girls

The mission of our club is to promote student empathy and awareness for the elderly in the Armonk community. Glamour Girls is a nationally recognized foundation. Through participation in this club, students will help seniors restore a sense of dignity while providing companionship. Although we are not able to go to the Bristal in the current climate of the world, we plan on connecting with the seniors in a safe and exciting way. We are looking forward to a great year!

Our first meeting will be Wednesday, September 22nd after school. More information about the meeting will be posted on Google Classroom (4rea3dq). If you are not able to attend but are interested, please email levys22@byramhills.net and bernsteinc23@byramhills.net.
